Our Classes

Zumba Toning

This variation adds lightweight toning sticks or dumbbells to the workout, targeting specific muscle groups while still maintaining the fun and dynamic atmosphere of traditional Zumba.

Aqua Zumba

Conducted in a pool, Aqua Zumba combines traditional Zumba movements with water resistance, offering a low-impact workout that is easy on the joints.

Zumba Gold

Designed for older adults or those who are new to exercise, Zumba Gold modifies the moves and pacing of traditional Zumba to make it accessible to a wider range of participants.
