Our Classes

Long Distance Running

This involves running at a steady pace over long distances, typically more than 5 kilometers (3.1 miles). Long-distance running builds endurance and cardiovascular fitness.

Sprinting

Sprinting involves running at maximum speed over short distances, usually less than 400 meters. Sprinting helps to develop speed, power, and explosive strength.

Recovery Running

Recovery runs are done at a relaxed, easy pace to promote recovery from more intense workouts. They help to improve blood flow, reduce muscle soreness, and enhance overall recovery..
